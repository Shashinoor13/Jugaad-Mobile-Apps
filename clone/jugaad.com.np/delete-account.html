How to Delete Your Jugaad App Account

If you wish to delete your account from the Jugaad app, follow the steps below. Please note that this action is irreversible, and all your data associated with the account will be permanently deleted.

Steps to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the Jugaad App: Log in to your account on the Jugaad app using your registered credentials.
  2. Navigate to the Profile Tab: Tap on the Profile tab, located at the bottom navigation bar of the app.
  3. Select Delete Account: Scroll down to find the Delete Account option. Tap on it to proceed.
  4. Confirm Deletion: You will be prompted to confirm the account deletion. Read the message carefully and tap on Confirm to delete your account.

Need Help?
